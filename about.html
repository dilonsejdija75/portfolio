My Journey

My journey into web development began during my college years, where I discovered my passion for creating digital experiences. What started as curiosity about how websites work quickly evolved into a deep fascination with frontend technologies.

I specialise in frontend development using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript - the core technologies I fell in love with. There's something magical about transforming ideas into interactive, responsive websites that users can engage with across any device.

Over the past 1.5 years, I've dedicated myself to mastering these fundamental technologies, believing that a strong foundation in the basics leads to better, more maintainable code. I enjoy crafting clean, responsive websites and turning complex problems into simple, beautiful designs.
